Hot Honey Grilled Chicken Thighs

Ingredients:

  • 8 bone-in chicken thighs
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken thighs with salt and black pepper to taste.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the hot sauce, honey, olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Brush the chicken thighs generously with the hot honey mixture on both sides.
  5. Place the chicken thighs on the preheated grill, skin side down. Grill for about 6-7 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
  6. Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes and enjoy your delicious 5-star hot honey grilled chicken thighs!
